Here’s How a Mobile-Friendly Website Can Help You Live a Better Life

Take a look at these mobile marketing statistics (from December 2014) and see how mobile web is at the heart of the mobile revolution.

  • 94% of CMOs plan to use mobile applications within the next 3-5 years. Marketing Land
  • Over half of all mobile searches lead to a purchase. Rocket Post
  • Email is the most popular activity on smartphones among users ages 18-44. Mark the Marketer
  • Mobile is the channel of choice for keeping relationships with existing customers alive because it cuts through the clutter of email and social. Forbes
  • 71% of users access social media from a mobile device. Jeff Bullas
  • 50% of millenials use smartphones to research products or services while shopping, and 41% have made purchases across their phones. Leaderwest Digital Marketing Journal

Companies should take advantage of the fact that smartphones are now a necessity to most people. Constant communication and access to digital information has become the way of living.

Some companies developed apps that users can download, but how about those who just like to surf the web?

How can they purchase from their favorite online store, or order food, or get directions when the website is difficult to read from a phone?

Having a mobile-friendly website is about providing easy accessibility for your users.

Happy customers = successful business right?

Regardless of what goals you have set for your website, there are a lot of hidden benefits to convert a desktop website to a mobile-friendly version.
